Who is Cheng Feng Sun?

I am a fast-paced learning enthusiast and a decisive go-getter. I think graphically and sketch down any ideas.

Coming from a background in business and information management, I focus on delivering elegant, high usability solutions that meet the needs of the users and the business. Information empowers people. I'm passionate about bringing providing accessibility to others.

What got you into UX?

"I love mankind...it people I can't stand. " - Linus van Pelt.

I've always had a passion for mankind and design (I find people interesting too). Looking back, I grew up drawing my classmates and teachers on textbooks; I enjoy listening to others more than talking; I get excited looking at what people put into their designs and how others interact with it; all problems are people problems, and I love solving them; all of which is similar to what UX designers do - observe people and humanize technology, I just didn't know it then.

What connected the dots was when I was a research assistant at my university, National Chengchi University in Taiwan. Slowly, I was exposed to more user behavior studies. It's fascinating how behind numbers and pictures, there are users whom we are trying to make technology more usable, enjoyable, and accessible for.
